H.E. Peng Ly Sinek, Deputy Director General and Secretary-General of the Digital Skills Development Program, was invited as a guest speaker at the event titled “Meet & Greet Grade A, B and C Students (2025) with Mony.”

Phnom Penh, On the afternoon of October 18, 2025, H.E. Peng Ly Sinek, Deputy Director General and Secretary-General of the Digital Skills Development Program (DSDP), was invited as a guest speaker at the event titled “Meet & Greet Grade A, B, and C Students (2025) with Mony”, held at the American University of Phnom Penh (AUPP).

The event was also joined by speakers from various companies, representatives from AUPP, and around 200 students who achieved Grade A, B, and C in the 2025 national exam.

The purpose of this event was to:

 - Highlight the benefits of education,

 - Share tips on obtaining scholarships to study abroad, and

 - Promote the Techo Digital Talent Scholarship and the Digital Skills Development Program.

During the program, several speakers gave presentations, including H.E. Peng Ly Sinek, who spoke about the advantages of the Digital Skills Development Program. She emphasized the program’s commitment to empowering students to pursue their dreams in the field of digital technology, while also ensuring sustainability through continued financial support for future generations of students.

She further noted that this initiative contributes to expanding scholarship opportunities across various platforms aimed at building and developing human resources in the digital sector — a crucial need, as Cambodia still faces a shortage of skilled digital professionals compared to neighboring countries in the ASEAN region.

As the number of digital professionals increases, Cambodia will be better positioned to strengthen its competitiveness in the digital economy regionally and globally.

For more information, please visit the official website of the Digital Skills Development Program at www.dsdp.gov.kh
 to stay updated on the latest developments.
