Phnom Penh, On the afternoon of April 23, 2026, at the Ministry of Post and Telecommunications (MPTC), H.E. Mao Sila, Undersecretary of State of MPTC, presided over a meeting to discuss the expansion of partnerships for digital technology training through the Techo Digital Talent Scholarship and the Digital Skills Development Program (DSDP).

The meeting was attended by representatives from the Cambodia Academy of Digital Technology (CADT), the Chairwoman and members of the DSDP Secretariat, and representatives from the University of Cambodia (UC), with a total of 11 participants.

The purpose of the meeting was to further explore collaboration opportunities with the University of Cambodia, with the aim of expanding partnerships with MPTC in the field of digital technology training.

To date, MPTC has provided Techo Digital Talent Scholarships and interest-free financial support through the DSDP to students studying at six partner higher education institutions, namely: CADT, the Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC), the Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP), the National University of Management (NUM), the American University of Phnom Penh (AUPP), and Paragon International University.

For more information, please visit the official website of the DSDP at: www.dsdp.gov.kh to stay updated on the latest developments.
