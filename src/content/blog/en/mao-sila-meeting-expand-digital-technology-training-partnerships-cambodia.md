His Excellency Mao Sila chaired a meeting to discuss expanding partnerships in digital technology training.

Phnom Penh: On the afternoon of April 23, 2026, at the Ministry of Post and Telecommunications (MPTC), His Excellency Mao Sila, Undersecretary of State of MPTC, presided over a meeting to discuss the expansion of partnerships in digital technology training through the Techo Digital Talent Scholarship and the Digital Skills Development Program (DSDP).

The meeting was attended by representatives from the Cambodia Academy of Digital Technology (CADT), the Chairwoman and members of the DSDP Secretariat, and representatives from the University of Cambodia (UC), with a total of 11 participants.

The purpose of this meeting was to further explore collaboration opportunities with the University of Cambodia, aiming to expand partnerships with MPTC in digital technology training.

To date, MPTC has provided Techo Digital Talent Scholarships and interest-free financial support through the DSDP to students studying at six partner higher education institutions, including CADT, the Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC), the Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP), the National University of Management (NUM), the American University of Phnom Penh (AUPP), and Paragon International University.

For more information, please visit the official website of DSDP at www.dsdp.gov.kh to follow the latest updates.
